How to enter:
You must write a javascript function that will control your snake through the grid and make it survive for as long as possible. It cannot collide with the walls or itself or its opponent. Each iteration, your AI function will be called and must return one of four strings: "n", "s", "e", or "w", for North, South, East, and West. Case does matter. Your function has four variables at its disposal:
  • map: an array of array of length-1 strings, "#" for wall, "1" for red snake, "2"for blue snake, and " " for empty.
  • side: one of either "1" or "2" representing which snake you are
  • pos: an object with an x property and a y property representing the coordinates of your current snake's head
  • enemypos: a similar object for your opponent
Please store all of your global variables in vars[side].
